“Computing and Culture”

Refactor Camp 2014 will be held and March 1-2 at the Computer History Museum in Mountain View, CA. The theme this year is "Computing and Culture" and we'll discuss everything from bitcoin and geek fashion to inequality and basic income. This time, unlike previous years, we'll be attempting a pure barcamp format, with lightning talks, small-group sessions and full-room sessions proposed and organized by attendees.
